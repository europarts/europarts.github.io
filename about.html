ABOUT US

We are a Veteran Owned, family-run, used European vehicle parts business focusing on Land Rover, Porsche, and Audi. Additionally, we are happy to work with you to build your dream Overland vehicle on your desired platform. We were established in May 2021 and are located in Grovetown GA.

DEVELOPMENT

Over the last 20 years, having owned many high-end SUVs, I have come to realize the cost of repairs is what really ruins the ownership experience of these wonderful vehicles. Being a DIY person, helped eliminate the labor cost, but the high parts costs left me reeling. To that end, I sought out used parts to keep my trucks on the road. This provides a lower cost option for the correct part, and in many cases one that is no longer even available in the new parts market.

Looking Forward

Most used parts companies require a phone call or email to find out if they have what you need. We are working towards having our full inventory on one website that allows you to browse, see the exact part you are getting, and its description without that wait to find out if we have it. We are planning by fall of 2022 to be fully operational with an interactive web experience. The site will allow you to browse our inventory, see ongoing builds, and trail reports from off road or overland adventures we have taken.

Company Goals

We will continue to strive to provide fast, friendly service, at a reasonable price point. To take care of our customers to the best of our ability, helping them to “Maintain Your Adventure”. We look forward to seeing you at many of the events we attend each year within the Off Road community.
